What is an Akan name?

The Akan, culturally and linguistically related tribes in West Africa, do the same as other African and Jamaican ethnic groups: Name their children after the respective day they were born. Over time, the Akan, particularly in Ghana, have defined seven names per gender, each referring to a day of the week:

Table of Akan Names
